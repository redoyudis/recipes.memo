Everything here are easy, healthy, with only less than three inggridient with little to no effort.

about author

LAZY MEATBALL

inggridients

  • 250g of Ground Beef
  • One whole Onion

steps

  1. Chop whole onion to small pieces
  2. Season the ground beef with salt, paper, chilli powder, garlic powder,and plenty of msg
  3. Mix the copped onion with the ground beef
  4. Make a small ball with the mixed ground beef
  5. Cook the small balls in the air fryer for 10 minutes in 180 degrees fahrenheit
